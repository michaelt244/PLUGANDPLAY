Marketing Section — 3 Approaches

How should the ad generator + dispatch flow be structured?

A

Linear Wizard (Recommended)

Step-by-step flow: Upload Photo → Pick Ad Variant → Select Platforms → Manus Dispatches. One screen per step, progress bar at top. Crystal clear story for judges — they watch the whole journey unfold.

Pros

  • Best demo narrative
  • Easy to follow live
  • Feels polished fast

Cons

  • Can't jump between steps
  • Less "product" feel
B

Dashboard with Tabs

Full product dashboard: Scan | Generate | Dispatch | Track | Retain tabs. Shows the whole platform scope at a glance. More impressive as a product vision.

Pros

  • Shows full platform
  • Real product feel
  • Easy to navigate

Cons

  • More to build
  • Demo story less linear
C

Single Campaign Builder

Everything on one scrollable page: community insights on left, ad editor in center, platform dispatch queue on right. Power-user feel, shows all data at once.

Pros

  • Dense, impressive layout
  • Shows data + action together

Cons

  • Complex to build well
  • Overwhelming for a demo
